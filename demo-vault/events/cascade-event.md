# The Cascade Event

A catastrophic failure at the [[Helios Institute]] that occurred during [[Elena Vance]]'s experiments with [[Quantum Resonance Theory]].

**Date**: March 15th, 2024
**Location**: [[New Meridian]], Eastern Sector
**Casualties**: Officially 47, suspected higher

The event was triggered by an overload in the [[Resonance Chamber]]. [[Marcus Chen]] claims it was an accident. [[Sofia Reyes]] believes it was deliberate sabotage by [[The Syndicate]].

[[Dr. Sarah Webb]] predicted this event in her suppressed research papers.
