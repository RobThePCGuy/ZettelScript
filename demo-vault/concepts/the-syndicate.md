# The Syndicate

A shadowy organization with deep connections to government, corporate, and academic institutions.

**Known Activities**:
- Secret funding of the [[Helios Institute]]
- Research into [[Quantum Resonance Theory]] dating back decades
- Operations in [[The Underground]] beneath [[New Meridian]]
- Possible involvement in the [[Cascade Event]]

**Key Connections**:
- [[Marcus Chen]] (suspected liaison)
- [[Blackwood University]] (through research grants)
- [[The Forgotten Archives]] (historical records)

[[Sofia Reyes]] has been building a case against the organization. [[Dr. Sarah Webb]]'s testimony could expose them.
