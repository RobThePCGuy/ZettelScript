# Helios Institute

A cutting-edge research facility located in [[New Meridian]]. Founded by [[The Syndicate]] but publicly operated as an independent institution.

Houses the [[Resonance Chamber]] where [[Elena Vance]] conducts her experiments on [[Quantum Resonance Theory]].

Key personnel:
- [[Marcus Chen]] (Director)
- [[Elena Vance]] (Lead Researcher)
- [[Dr. Sarah Webb]] (Former Theoretical Lead)

The institute was ground zero for the [[Cascade Event]].
