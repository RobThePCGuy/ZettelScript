# Sofia Reyes

Federal investigator assigned to the [[Cascade Event]]. Former friend of [[Elena Vance]] from their time at [[Blackwood University]].

Sofia suspects [[Marcus Chen]] of covering up the true death toll at [[New Meridian]]. She's been tracking [[The Syndicate]]'s involvement with the [[Helios Institute]].

Her investigation led her to [[The Forgotten Archives]] beneath [[Old Town]].
