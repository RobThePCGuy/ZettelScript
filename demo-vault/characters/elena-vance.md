# Elena Vance

Lead researcher at the [[Helios Institute]]. Pioneer of [[Quantum Resonance Theory]].

Elena discovered the [[Cascade Event]] during her experiments with the [[Resonance Chamber]]. She believes [[Marcus Chen]] is hiding something about the true nature of the research.

Her relationship with [[Sofia Reyes]] has been strained since the incident at [[New Meridian]].
