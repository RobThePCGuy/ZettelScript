# Marcus Chen

Director of the [[Helios Institute]]. Former colleague of [[Elena Vance]] at [[Blackwood University]].

Marcus approved the construction of the [[Resonance Chamber]] despite warnings from [[Dr. Sarah Webb]]. He maintains connections to [[The Syndicate]] through back channels.

Present during the [[Cascade Event]]. His testimony contradicts [[Sofia Reyes]]'s account.
