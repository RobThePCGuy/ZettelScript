# Dr. Sarah Webb

Theoretical physicist who warned about the dangers of [[Quantum Resonance Theory]]. Colleague of [[Elena Vance]].

Sarah's research notes predicted the [[Cascade Event]] six months before it happened. She was silenced by [[Marcus Chen]] and reassigned to [[Blackwood University]].

Her disappearance from [[New Meridian]] remains unsolved. [[Sofia Reyes]] believes she's hiding in [[The Underground]].
